"Scan the World" is an open-source museum that offers a vast archive of 18,000 digital scans of significant sculptures and cultural artifacts from art history. This collection includes renowned works like the "Bust of Nefertiti," Rodin’s "The Thinker," and Michelangelo’s "David," all available for free download and 3D printing. COLOSSAL

Users can search the collection by artist, location, or collection, and each artifact's page provides historical context, location details, and technical specifications such as dimensions and estimated print time. The platform has collaborated with Google Arts and Culture to expand its offerings, adding thousands of additional pieces.
