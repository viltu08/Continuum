Fold

Book Folding Techniques by Paul Jackson explores the art and craft of book folding, where pages of books are folded in intricate patterns to create stunning designs. The book provides step-by-step instructions and detailed illustrations for a variety of techniques, including pleats, spirals, and sculptural forms. It also delves into the creative possibilities of turning books into three-dimensional works of art, catering to both beginners and experienced paper artists. This guide combines artistic inspiration with practical know-how, making it an essential resource for anyone interested in paper craft and book artistry.
