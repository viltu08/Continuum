The Shape of Design by Frank Chimero is a thoughtful exploration of the creative process and the broader impact of design on culture and society. Rather than focusing on technical skills or software tutorials, the book delves into the philosophical and emotional aspects of creating. Chimero examines how designers shape their work and, in turn, how their work shapes the world and its audiences. Through a blend of essays and poetic reflections, the book encourages readers to consider storytelling, intuition, and empathy as essential elements of design.
