"Dictionary of the Possible" is the 22nd issue of Shifter Magazine, edited by Sreshta Rit Premnath and Avi Alpert. This publication compiles a series of public discussions held over a year, each centered on unraveling a keyword that carries both urgency and agency in contemporary discourse. Instead of offering static definitions, the dictionary catalogs these keywords alongside questions provoked during each discussion, aiming to incite ongoing dialogue and exploration.

Contributors include artists, writers, activists, and philosophers, reflecting a diverse and interdisciplinary approach to understanding and expanding the possible.
