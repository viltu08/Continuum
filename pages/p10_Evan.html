Sequential

Evan M. Cohen's sequential layouts are a hallmark of his storytelling, blending the visual language of comic art with poetic rhythm. His panels flow seamlessly, guiding the viewer's eye through narratives that unfold with a meditative pace. Cohen uses layout to evoke emotions, often incorporating negative space, shifting perspectives, and symbolic transitions that mirror the emotional and thematic progression of the story.

The sequential design in his work isnâ€™t bound by conventional comic grid structures. Instead, Cohen often uses fluid, organic compositions where panels merge into one another, creating a sense of continuity and movement. This approach allows for a dynamic interplay between moments of stillness and action, amplifying the impact of key visual and narrative beats.

His layouts emphasize the connection between characters and their environments, often weaving elements of nature and the cosmos into the storytelling. This creates a sense of unity between the physical and emotional realms, making the sequential flow feel as intuitive as it is immersive.
