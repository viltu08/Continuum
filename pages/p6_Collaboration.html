The article "Collaboration, Cultural Nuance + Localism—A Conversation on Reshaping the Design Monolith" from AIGA's Eye on Design features a dialogue with three designers: Kresna Dwitomo of Projek Agni in Indonesia, Ivorian artist and designer O’Plerou Grebet, and New Delhi-based designer Ishan Khosla. They discuss integrating local visual cultures into their design processes, challenging the dominance of Eurocentric design paradigms. The conversation explores how design can transcend commercial objectives to narrate personal stories, rich histories, and dynamic local realities, emphasizing the importance of cultural authenticity and collaboration in creating meaningful design solutions.
